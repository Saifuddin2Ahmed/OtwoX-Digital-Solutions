## About

**OtwoX Digital Solutions** is an enterprise software company building proprietary business platforms for the markets the rest of the industry has overlooked — Africa, the Middle East, and the Arabic-speaking world.

We build everything in-house on **OtwoX OS**, our proprietary enterprise platform, with **OtwoX Engine** powering the AI and automation layer and **OtwoX Modules** delivering the industry-specific functionality our clients depend on. Founded by an Arab control engineer for businesses that have spent too long forcing themselves into software designed for somewhere else.

---

## Services

- **OtwoX OS Deployments** — full ERP rollouts on our proprietary platform with bilingual Arabic/English UX, multi-currency, and offline-tolerant operations baked into the core
- **OtwoX Modules** — sector-specific business capabilities for NGOs, humanitarian organizations, healthcare networks, manufacturing, and professional services
- **OtwoX AI Workforce** — autonomous AI agents powered by OtwoX Engine that handle reconciliation, customer support, document processing, and operational automation
- **Custom Integrations** — connecting OtwoX OS to the payment rails, communication channels, and government systems that operate in our markets
- **White-Label Platforms** — multi-tenant deployments for NGO networks and franchise businesses, branded as the partner's own product
